🏎️ How to Play SonicDrive

🚗 Lane Movement

Move RIGHT 🤜➡️

Give a quick glimpse of opening your RIGHT hand (brief flash), then close it again immediately. Car moves to the right lane.

Move LEFT ⬅️🤛

Give a quick glimpse of opening your LEFT hand (brief flash), then close it again immediately. Car moves to the left lane.

⚡ Speed Control

ACCELERATE 🏎️💨

Extend your RIGHT INDEX FINGER (☝️ right hand). Keep it extended while you want to accelerate. Other fingers stay closed.

BRAKE 🛑

Extend your LEFT INDEX FINGER (☝️ left hand). Keep it extended while you want to brake. Other fingers stay closed.
